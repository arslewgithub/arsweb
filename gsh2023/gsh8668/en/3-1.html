The Gods worshiped at Wanhe Temple

At the beginning of the founding of the Wanhe Temple, there was only one deity dedicated to Mazu (The Goddess of Sea), and after that, there were four other Goddessses were invited into the temple. Besides, the Goddess of Sea was open-minded to share the huge temple with many Chinese Gods, who can be seen at the temple now.
