Sacred beasts in “The Classic of Mountains and Seas”

Most of the charming mythical creatures were originally from China, and were published in “The Classic of Mountains and Seas ,” which was published in 475 BC, about 2496 years ago. Its contents include not just geography, mountains, rivers, and bizarre mountains and valleys, but also all kinds of Sacred Beasts.
