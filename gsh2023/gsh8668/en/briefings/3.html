We contacted and discussed with our instructors via e-mail and Lines. Besides, through browsing the web pages on line, we knew who we could consult to get the information we needed.
Although the " Wanhe Temple " near our school has been a famous historical site, few people in our country know or visit it. The impacts the research project has imposed on us:

During the process of exploring our hometown, we found many unique historical stories. We have also achieved our diverse abilities, including cooperation, communication, writing, film-making and design thinking, which are the most invaluable treasure we acquire in the project.

Respect intellectual property rights and how we quote legally

To respect intellectual property is the universal consensus. Our team is always responsible to comply with it. At the time of making the website, we used all the available self-reported images. When applying to the movie " Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings ", we would send notices to the Film Company to inform them of the ways we used their pictures.

Discovery, lesson, and surprises

When attending the project, we are surprised that there are so many interesting sacred beasts appearing not only in the Hollywood movie but also in the local temple near our school.
Thankfully, after finishing the project, all the members in our team get better capabilities in time management and communication, which we believe would help us a lot in tackling everything in the future.
